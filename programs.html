Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Valid entrance exam score (where applicable)

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks
  • Valid entrance exam score (GATE/CAT/etc.)

Diploma Programs

  • 10th or 12th pass from recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Basic computer knowledge preferred
